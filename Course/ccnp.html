CCNP Course Details

At Corvit Systems, we have designed a comprehensive CCNP training course. It covers all the topics required to pass the certification exam and prepares you to deal with complex networks. This course prepares students with knowledge and skills to deal with all issues related to LAN and WAN for enterprises. Some key topics our trainers cover when conducting CCNP training classes are switching, automation and programmability, wireless networking, switching, routing, and many others.

Overall, we can divide the CCNP training course into two huge parts. These are the core and concentration exams. Let us explore them separately!

The Core Exam

It is the first part, and we can cover some basic and necessary concepts and technologies that matter the most in enterprise networking. Some of them are:

  • Virtualization
  • Automation and Programmability
  • Network design principles
  • Security
  • Network assurance
  • Layer 2 and layer 3 technologies

The Concentration Exam

It is the second part of the CCNP Enterprise training course. With this training, the learners become specialized individuals in enterprise networking and its related topics. Some of the concentration exams we offer at Corvit Systems are:

  • Enterprise Advanced Routing and Services
  • Enterprise Wireless Networks
  • Designing Enterprise Networks
